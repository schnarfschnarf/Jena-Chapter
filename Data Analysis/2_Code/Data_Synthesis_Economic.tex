\section{Preface}\label{preface}

This R Script is intended to be used to load, explore, and synthesize
the datasets for the following metrics measured in the Jena main
experiment: Aboveground biomass production, C\&N content in the soil,
Realized Species Richness, climate variability, leaching N (nitrogen
leaching), pollination, flower diversity, total flowering period per
plot, above- and belowground herbivory (for belowground measured as
energy fluxes in the food web), soil organic carbon (SOC), leaf dry
matter content (LDMC), soil diversity, microbial biomass + respiration,
